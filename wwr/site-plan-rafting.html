Overview

Purpose

Make the white water rafting guided services easily accessible and findable by anyone looking to learn what equipment is needed; previous preparations, and precautions to be taken, along with pricing, available schedules, and options to acquire services.

Audience

People 10 years old or more. Middle or upper class people that enjoy outdoor activities involving some adrenaline. May be people on vacation or looking for a short day-off memorable activity. People that needs to go out of the ordinary for about an hour or so. People that enjoy feeling that rush of adrenaline from doing something extreme, but safe at the same time. The site will be accesible via mobile phones, laptops, desktops, or even tablets.
